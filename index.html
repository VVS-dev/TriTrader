FAQ

Questions traders ask first

Straight answers on how it works, where the money comes from, and why it's not what you think.

Is this gambling?

No. TriTrader is a skill-based competition — your result comes entirely from your trading decisions on live market data, not chance. Every trade is simulated against real-time prices.

How do I know this is legit?

The prize pool is funded by entry fees and the full payout structure is published upfront — nothing is hidden. TriTrader is UK-based, and the rules are the same for every trader in the field.

Where does the prize money come from?

Entirely from entry fees, like a poker tournament. Around 45% of every entry flows straight back to players through the prize pool and bounties.

What happens if I don't win?

You only ever risk your entry fee, because every trade is simulated. The top 15% of the field cash out, with a minimum of 1.5× your entry. The other 85% walk away with sharper instincts — but no money beyond the entry fee they paid — no blown accounts, no surprises.

How is this different from a prop firm challenge?

No rules to follow and no evaluation. One week instead of months, real prize money instead of a funded account, and it rewards pure performance — not how well you stick to someone else's risk limits.

What can I trade?

Forex, indices, commodities, crypto and stocks — all in one competition, priced on real-time data. Trade the market you know best, or range across all of them.

What does joining the waitlist do?

It gets you early access. There's no payment now — we'll notify you before the first competition opens so you're first in line.
